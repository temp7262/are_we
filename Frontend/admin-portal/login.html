About This Portal

The JDCOEM Digital Document Services Admin Portal is the secure backend platform for authorised staff to review, approve, and issue academic documents requested by students.

Administrator Roles
Clerk
Initial document verification & forwarding to HOD
Head of Department (HOD)
Academic review, remarks & digital signature
Principal
Final approval authority & document issuance
Bonafide Certificate
Character Certificate
Leaving / Transfer Certificate
Official Transcript
No Objection Certificate (NOC)
Important Notice

This portal is for authorised JDCOEM administrative staff only. Unauthorised access attempts are logged and may result in disciplinary action.

Use your official college email and registered password. For account issues, contact IT at itsupport@jdcoem.ac.in.
